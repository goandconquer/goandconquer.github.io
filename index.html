Mustering the Mystifying Muses

Welcome to the homepage of my website! It's a pleasure to have you here. This is a landing pad for the colorful and misinformed diatribes of a computer-geek-turned-storyologist. Topics include programming, game development, creative writing, table-top RPGs and any other nerd-centric hobbies one might adopt. Often times, these items are left to gather dust just waiting for the right place to call home. I finally found the courage to create that place. While what you might find here isn't as flashy or sophisticated as other blogsites you've seen, I believe this place represents a crucial first step in establishing myself as a brand. Feel free to take a look around and browse what I have to offer. Don't worry, nothing here costs money or requires forced participation.
